# Jordan Rudess — full profile (researched 2026-07-11)

Operational lens: `skills/review-as/references/jordan.md`

## Verified background
Dream Theater keyboardist; founder of Wizdom Music (MorphWiz, SampleWiz, Geo Synthesizer). Co-created GeoShred with moForte — physical-modeling synthesis based on Julius O. Smith III's Stanford/CCRMA waveguide research; 2017 Electronic Musician Editor's Choice; GeoSWAM physically-modeled instruments. MPE advocate (Osmose demos). MIT collaboration on jam_bot (real-time human-AI duet, 2026 coverage). Publicly pro-AI in music.

- https://www.jordanrudess.com/wizdom-music/ · https://www.moforte.com/ · https://midi.org/jordan-rudess-on-geoshred-expressive-midi-and-the-future-of-music-tech · https://arts.mit.edu/jordan-rudess-mit/

## Taste profile
Arguably the most tech-engaged virtuoso keyboardist alive. Everything he builds is about maximal expressivity and playability: per-note pitch bend/vibrato/slides, continuous control, immediate tactile response, organic physically-modeled sound that "bends, stretches" like a real instrument. Demands real-time responsiveness.

## Would praise / would attack
Praise: true per-note expressivity (MPE-friendly); physically-modeled organic response; ultra-low latency; playability under virtuosic passages; browser accessibility bringing music-making to more people.
Attack: sluggish latency; dead on/off notes; no expression dimensions; static once triggered; anything that feels like a fixed sample player.

## Notes
GeoShred's success is proof-of-market for expressive waveguide PM on consumer devices — the closest commercial cousin to this project's synthesis approach.
