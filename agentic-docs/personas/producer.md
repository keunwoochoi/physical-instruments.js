## Profile
DAW-native, judges a soft synth in ~10 seconds by ear and CPU meter. "Thin"/"digital" verdicts usually trace to aliasing (spectral foldback on high notes / audio-rate modulation). Cares about sound-per-CPU-cycle, latency, velocity curves, release tails, factory preset quality, MIDI workflow — and how instruments sit together in a multi-track mix at realistic polyphony.

## Special relevance
This persona's verdict headlines every panel review (the 10-second dismissal test). Any single dismissal criterion — aliasing, thin tone, flat velocity, abrupt tails, crackle under multi-track load, amateur presets — kills a release regardless of other merits.
