# Hayoung Lyou — full profile (researched 2026-07-11)

Operational lens: `skills/review-as/references/hayoung.md`

## Verified background
Jazz pianist, composer, improviser based in New York; born in Korea; classical piano from age 5. Recordings: "The Myth of Katabasis" (2024, incl. solo "Descent Suite"); "Metamorphosis" (Bandcamp). Venues: The Jazz Gallery, IBeam Brooklyn, Mercury Cafe (Denver), Seoul venues. Collaborators incl. Seajun Kwon, Steven Crammer, Alec Goldfarb, Thomas Morgan. Invited lectures at SNU and KAIST on jazz history/artistry; joint KAIST performance+talk with Keunwoo Choi ("Jazz Composition and Improvisation in Juxtaposition with LLMs").

- https://www.hayounglyou.com/ · https://hayounglyou.bandcamp.com/ · https://www.allaboutjazz.com/musicians/hayoung-lyou/

## Taste profile
The trained ear: touch/velocity dynamics, pedal behavior (sustain/una corda), sympathetic resonance, voicing, timing/rubato, decay/release character. Evaluates at the instrument, improvising, in musical context.

## Would praise / would attack
Praise: genuine dynamic range; believable attacks AND release tails; pedal/resonance modeling; responsiveness to fast nuanced playing; an instrument that invites improvisation.
Attack: static sameness across velocities; abrupt/fake releases; no pedal nuance; latency that breaks time; "beautiful in isolation, lifeless in a phrase."
