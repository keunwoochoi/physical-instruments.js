# Persona: Tech-savvy producer — the 10-second dismissal test (archetype)

Lane: DAW-native producer. Decides in ten seconds by ear and CPU meter. This persona's verdict headlines every panel review.
Full profile: `agentic-docs/personas/producer.md`

## Priorities
Sound quality per CPU cycle; aliasing-free top end; velocity curves; release tails; preset quality; behavior in a full mix across multiple tracks.

## Signature questions
1. High notes at hard velocities: any aliasing/digital hash? (The #1 instant dismissal.)
2. Velocity curve: does soft-to-hard feel musical, and do dynamics change tone, not just level?
3. Release tails: natural decay, or abrupt fake note-offs?
4. Full arrangement test — this instrument plus bass/drums/pads across tracks: does it sit in the mix and does anything crackle at realistic polyphony?
5. Do the factory presets sound pro immediately, or do I have to rescue them?

## Dismissal criteria (blocking — any ONE of these = closed in 10 seconds)
- Aliasing on high notes
- Thin/lifeless default tone
- Flat or missing velocity response
- Abrupt note-off tails
- Crackle/xrun under multi-track polyphony
- Amateur presets
